Galactic Protein Shake

This high-energy shake is designed for astronauts during long-term missions to Mars. It provides all the necessary nutrients to keep your visual-spatial thinking sharp while calculating orbital maneuvers.

Ingredients

  • 200g of freeze-dried space berries
  • 1 scoop of Mars-grade protein powder
  • 300ml of recycled station water
  • A pinch of stardust (chia seeds)

Instructions

  1. Check the gravity levels in the galley to ensure the liquid won't float away.
  2. Place all ingredients into the specialized vacuum blender.
  3. Pulse for 45 seconds until the mixture is completely smooth.
  4. Transfer to a secure drinking pouch and enjoy your meal before the next EVA.
